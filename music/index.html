About

Anita Tse is a Vancouver-based musician specialized in singing and ukulele. She is known for performing unique renditions of well-loved songs, with a wide repertoire that spans from 60’s classics all the way to 2020’s pop and Tiktok songs. Her musical style is influenced by indie folk, folk pop, broadway, and jazz, and she aspires to leave her listeners feeling warm and uplifted.
