April 2017 - April 2020

University of British Columbia

Background

nwPlus is a student club at the University of British Columbia dedicated to fostering the hacker community and organizing multiple hackathons on campus. Most notably, nwPlus is the team behind nwHacks (Western Canada's largest hackathon), UBC Local Hack Day (the largest Local Hack Day in North America), and cmd-f (Vancouver's first and largest all-female* hackathon).

The nwPlus Team at cmd-f 2020

During my three years at nwPlus, I took on roles at each level of the team structure as nwHacks Logistics Coordinator, nwHacks Logistics Director, and most recently, Co-President.

The team structure

The nwPlus Team has subteams covering the different areas of hackathon organization including marketing, logistics, engagement, sponsorship, development, and design. Apart from three event-specific logistics subteams, all of the other subteams at nwPlus work throughout the year to support all three events to ensure consistency.

Involvement

As the nwHacks Logistics Team lead, I led a team of 5 members in the planning and execution of all logistics (Including venue, food, hacker experience, volunteers, external vendors) at nwHacks. My team introduced new inclusivity initiatives that have become standard in subsequent nwPlus events including having an all-gender bathroom, a safe space/prayer room, pronouns on nametags, and hosting a women* in tech event. This hackathon welcomed 700+ hackers, 100+ industry mentors, and 30+ sponsors from around the world to the University of British Columbia.
