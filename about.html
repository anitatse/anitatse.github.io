About me

I am a Computer Science graduate at the University of British Columbia, having experienced both Software Developer and DevOps Engineer internships at Copperleaf Technologies and Absolute Software.

I’m the outgoing Co-president of nwPlus UBC — the team behind Western Canada’s largest hackathon, nwHacks, North America’s largest Local Hack Day 2018, and Vancouver BC’s first all-female* 24-hour hackathon, cmd-f. I love fostering positive team relationships, leaping out of my comfort zone, and proving that tech can be for everyone.

When I’m not fixing builds and organizing hackathons, I enjoy catching up with friends, singing along to Broadway soundtracks, and channeling my inner Mulan through Chinese Cultural dance. 💃🏻

Feel free to reach out to me, let's grab a coffee! (or bubble tea?)
